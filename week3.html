During week 3 my class was tasked with three assignments:
1. Make a T-shirt
2. Complete a dye sublimination project
3. Print something on the UV printer.

We were required to make a T-shirt that went with the topic of either, Ohio, Western Reserve, or Hudson. I chose Ohio and made a collage of various sights and tourist attractions in Ohio.

Next, I made a mug with my logo on it for my sublimation project.

Lastly, UV printed a popsocket.

The shirt includes specific places and sights in Ohio like the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, WRA's Chapel, and the State Flag.



This was my dye sublimation project for the 3rd week of Digital Fabrication. We learned the advantages of sublimation over UV printing.
With dye sublimation, the color have a better chance of showing more vibrantly on the specific object being printed on, whether it be a shirt, mug, pencil case, neck tie, ect.
The number of the uses and variablity of products it can print on is vast, which makes it a much better industry.
