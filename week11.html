Final Project

For my final project I made a sign/light to hang on a wall or place on a desk/shelf of any sorts.
It is in the shape of a Les Paul guitar head with my logo and producer name laser cut into the top. Inside is a white LED backlight module.
It is all made from wood.

I used wood, a toggle switch, battery pack, LED, paint, and wood stain. Not to mention the lasers.

I spent almost every possible minute of my free time working on this project.
It took me about the entire rotation plus weekends, free periods, after school until 6, and A+.

The machines I used the most were the table saw and the laser engraver. For the laser engraver, I was using vectors to cut out the shape of the guitar and the hole for my switch as well as the decoration on the front.
The vector settings were varied between 8/10/100 and 2/20/100 depending on whether I was using 1/8 of an inch of wood or 1/4 of an inch.

I learned my way around Corel Draw a little better, and I learned how to use various tools such as drills, routers, and table saws.
