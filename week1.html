We kicked off the first week of Digital Fabrication and Engineering with group challenges.
The first challenge: Make the tallest structure soley made up of newspaper and tape that can withstand the force of a small fan on low power standing a few feet away.

Me and my partner attempted to make a structure with a stable base but ultimately failed as it was not
very tall or able to withstand the force of the fan even at its lowest setting.

The second challenge was to make something out of straws and 18 inches of tape. This particular structure had to stop a golf ball from bouncing
out after being dropped out of midair at a controlled height.
Only three straws were allowed to actually come into contact with the ground. My group decided to make a contraption that, if all went according to plan, would act as a triangular shaped box of some sorts.
Once again, my group failed.
